About Me

I'm a passionate frontend engineer with a keen eye for creating exceptional digital experiences. With a strong foundation in UI/UX design and modern web development, I specialize in building responsive, accessible, and scalable applications using React, Next.js, TypeScript, and TailwindCSS.
